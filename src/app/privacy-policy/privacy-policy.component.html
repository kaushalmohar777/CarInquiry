INFORMATION WE COLLECT :

Throughout your engagement with our website or services, we may collect personal information from both carriers and customers, which may include:

  1. Contact Information : This incorporates, but is not limited to, your name, address, email address, and phone number.
  2. Shipment Details : Information crucial to the transportation process, covering pick-up and delivery locations.
  3. Payment Information :Data necessary for billing and facilitating payment transactions. This includes payment preferences and methods.
  4. Communication Data : All correspondence and communication exchanged between you and Speedy Way Auto Transport. This encompasses messages, emails, and any other forms of communication related to the transportation services.
  5. Vehicle Details for Carriers : Carriers may be required to provide specific information about the vehicles they transport, including vehicle identification numbers (VINs), dimensions, weight, and other relevant details.
  6. Bill of Lading : For both carriers and customers, a Bill of Lading is issued at the point of origin and/or destination. This document details the condition of the shipment, serving as a receipt and, for carriers, as a contract of transportation. It includes information such as the type and condition of the vehicle, pick-up and delivery locations, and any pre-existing damage.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION :

At Speedy Way Auto Transport, the information we collect from both carriers and customers serves various essential purposes, including:

  1. Processing and Fulfilling Transportation Requests :
    1. - For Carriers : Your details, along with the specific information about the shipment, are used to match you with appropriate transportation requests and facilitate the transportation process.
    2. - For Customers : Your information aids in coordinating the transportation of your vehicle, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience.
  2. Communication Regarding Shipments : Carriers and customers will receive updates, notifications, and relevant communication regarding the status of their shipments. This includes information about pickup and delivery times, any delays, and other essential details.
  3. Customer Support and Inquiry Resolution : We use your information to provide effective customer support and address any inquiries or concerns you may have. This involves responding to messages, resolving issues, and ensuring a satisfactory experience.
  4. Service Improvement and User Experience Enhancement : Analyzing the data collected allows us to identify areas for improvement in our services. This may involve optimizing transportation processes, enhancing website functionality, and tailoring our services to better meet the needs of carriers and customers alike.
  5. Billing and Payment Processing : Information related to payment preferences, billing, and payment transactions is utilized for processing payments accurately and efficiently. This ensures a smooth financial transaction process for both carriers and customers.
  6. Marketing Communications : Your contact information, such as your email address, may be utilized to send you marketing communications. These communications could include newsletters, promotional offers, and updates about our services.
  7. Personalized Marketing : We may analyze the data we collect to provide personalized marketing content tailored to your preferences, interests, and usage patterns. This helps us enhance your overall experience and ensure the relevance of the information we share
  8. Targeted Advertising : Information related to your shipment details and preferences may be used to deliver targeted advertising. This could include advertisements for our services, promotions, or other relevant content across various platforms.

INFORMATION SHARING AND DISCLOSURE :

At Speedy Way Auto Transport, we prioritize the responsible handling of your personal information. Here's how we share and disclose such information:

  1. Third-Party Carriers : Your personal information, particularly shipment details, is shared with third-party carriers directly involved in the transportation of your vehicle. This is essential for coordinating and fulfilling transportation requests.
  2. Service Providers : We may engage service providers to assist in various aspects of our business operations, including payment processing and customer support. These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are authorized to use your information only for specified purposes.
  3. Legal Compliance : In certain situations, we may disclose your personal information to legal authorities when required to comply with the law or protect our rights. This includes situations where disclosure is necessary for regulatory compliance or responding to legal processes.
  4. No Sale or Rent of Personal Information : Speedy Way Auto Transport does not engage in the sale or rental of your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy is a priority, and your information is treated with the utmost confidentiality
